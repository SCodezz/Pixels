About Us

Welcome to our innovative platform, where learning about environmental sustainability is engaging, informative, and fun! We are a passionate team dedicated to addressing the critical issue of improper waste disposal among young children and young adults in India. Our mission is to create a transformative educational experience that raises awareness about waste management and empowers the younger generation to take action in their communities.

Main Game

Scan & Snap! Simply scan a QR code, upload a photo showing proof of your completed task, and watch as a check mark appears. Each task completion rewards you with virtual coins, making daily achievements fun and rewarding.

Mini Games

Stuck at home for the day? Why not turn your time into a fun and educational experience with our exciting range of recycling-themed mini games! As you play, you'll earn points that can be redeemed for virtual rewards, making your journey towards becoming a recycling hero even more rewarding. So, get started now and see how many points you can score!
